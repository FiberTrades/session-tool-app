Series of 10

See your edge in tens.

A single trade tells you nothing. Ten tells you plenty. Session Tool groups your trades into rolling series of ten so variance stops masquerading as skill — and so a bad run reads as a sample, not a verdict.

  • Win / loss / break-even mapped across each block of ten
  • Net R and account % per series
  • Series history so you can compare runs over time
  • Export a series summary straight to Discord

Risk Rules

Rules you set. Rules it holds you to.

Define five guardrails per account in percent or pounds. Session Tool watches the day against them — and when you cross one, the greeting that meets you doesn't congratulate you. It tells you, plainly.

  • Max drawdown, daily drawdown, risk per trade, profit target, max trades
  • Set each in % or £, per account
  • Live banners and greetings flag a breach as it happens
  • Works across multiple accounts and prop challenges
